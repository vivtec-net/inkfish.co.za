We've spent years behind the scenes, helping other brands find their voice, refine their message, and step into the spotlight.

But for a long time, we didn't do that for ourselves.

Yes, we had a logo. A website. A name that clients remembered.

But we knew that wasn't enough anymore.

We've grown. Evolved. Built stronger systems, a stronger team, and a clearer vision.

This rebrand wasn't a design refresh. It was a realignment with our purpose, our people, and our future.

So here it is.

Same Inkfish. New gear.

## So… Why Now?

Rebrands don't happen overnight. Ours certainly didn't.
It started with discomfort that nagging sense that our brand no longer reflected who we'd become. The work we were doing was bigger, sharper, more strategic. But you wouldn't know that from looking at our old presence.

We were proud of what we were delivering. But we weren't proud of how we were showing up.

So we took our own medicine. We slowed down. Revisited our core. Asked big questions:

- What kind of partner are we?
- Who do we really serve best?
- What makes Inkfish different and how do we prove that?

From there, everything changed.

## Not Just a New Look

This rebrand wasn't just cosmetic. It was structural. Foundational.
We rebuilt the way we think, the way we talk about our work, and how we show up in every space.
Here's what we redefined:

## Our Vision

To be the leading digital marketing partner for businesses by providing exceptional service, innovative solutions, and unwavering support.
We're not chasing the title of biggest.
We're focused on being the most trusted.
(But hey. If we end up being the biggest too… we won't complain 😉)

## Our Mission

To stay one step ahead, anticipating the needs of our clients and empowering them to grow with strategy, creativity, and heart.
We're not here to just “do the work.”
We're here to be your team.

## Our Core Values

- Excellence. Not optional, expected.
- Support. Clients first, always.
- Innovation. Strategy now, not six months late.
- Human-first. Real people. Real conversations.
- Accountability. We own the work. The good and the hard

## Rebuilding From the Inside, Too

- Alongside the brand, we invested in our people.
- We ran team sessions and strategy workshops.
- We revisited our strengths and redefined our roles.
- We held space for honest conversations and feedback.
- Because a strong brand starts with a strong team.
- And if we wanted to lead with clarity, we had to live it first.
- This rebrand is as much about culture as it is about communication.

### What You'll See Going Forward

A clearer message across our site, proposals, and pitches
Consistent content (finally!). Insights, updates, proof of work
A bolder presence on social media (less mystery, more voice)
More tools, support, and guidance for clients already working with us
A team that's energised, aligned, and all in

## And For Our Clients?

We're still Inkfish.

Still the same group of thinkers, designers, strategists, and builders.
But now we're doing it with more clarity, more collaboration and a brand that matches the quality of the work.

We're also using this moment to reconnect with you.

To look at what's next. What can be refined, added, sharpened, or scaled.
Because a rebrand isn't the end. It's a starting point.
So, whether you've been with us for years or just joined the journey, thank you.

We're glad you're here. And we're just getting started.
